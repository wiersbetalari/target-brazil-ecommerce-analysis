# 📊 Target Brazil E-commerce Sales & Operations Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes over 99,000 e-commerce orders from Target Brazil to uncover business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, logistics, payment trends, and regional operations.

The project combines SQL and Tableau to solve real-world business problems and present insights through an interactive dashboard.

## Key Business Insights

- Sales increased year-over-year.
- Credit Card is the dominant payment method.
- Significant regional revenue differences exist across states.
- Freight costs vary considerably between regions.
- Delivery performance differs across states.
- Seasonal demand patterns can support inventory planning.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Expand operations in high-performing states.
- Improve logistics in high freight-cost regions.
- Optimize delivery performance.
- Strengthen forecasting during seasonal demand.
- Promote flexible payment options.
